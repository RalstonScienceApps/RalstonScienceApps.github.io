1. Get Equipment
    • Track
    • Cart
    • Endstop
    • Track Feet
    • Cart Flag
    • Motion Detector
    • Motion Detector Bracket
    • LabQuest
  2. Set up the equipment as seen in the picture
  3. On the main screen of the LabQuest, click the box that says 'Mode' at the top
  4. Set the time to 15 seconds
  5. Click okay
  6. Hold the cart around 40 cm away from the motion detector.
  7. Press play
  8. As soon as you hear the clicking, release the cart down the ramp
