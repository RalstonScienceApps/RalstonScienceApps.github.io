Hypothesis

Create an if/then hypothesis that predicts if there will be more or less swings in 10 seconds as the pendulum gets shorter.

Procedure

  1. Click on "Intro"
  2. In the bottom left hand corner, turn off ruler and turn on stopwatch.
  3. Record the length of the pendulum in your data table
  4. Press the pause button and pull the pendulum back to 10Ëš.
  5. Press play and count the number of complete swings in 10 seconds.
  6. Record this in your data table.
  7. Make the pendulum shorter and repeat steps 3 through 6.
  8. Do this for three different lengths.
